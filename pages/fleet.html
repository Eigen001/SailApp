FAQ
Common questions
How does bulk vessel import work? ﹀
Upload a CSV with your vessel data — name, MMSI, HIN, hull description, home harbor. We validate against USCG documentation records and import all vessels simultaneously. Most marina imports complete in under 5 minutes.
Can individual vessel owners claim their boat within a fleet? ﹀
Yes. Any vessel owner can claim their boat by verifying ownership with USCG documentation. Claimed vessels get a personal profile linked to the fleet profile. Both the marina and the owner receive photo alerts.
What's included in the analytics dashboard? ﹀
Photo match volume by vessel, most-photographed vessels, harbor activity heat map, fleet engagement scores and a weekly email digest of top shots. Custom reports available on Enterprise.
Do you offer annual billing discounts? ﹀
Yes — annual billing saves 20% across all fleet tiers. Contact sales for annual invoicing or to discuss multi-year agreements on Enterprise.
