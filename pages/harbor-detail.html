About This Harbor
Mystic — crown jewel of the Connecticut coast

Mystic is the finest harbor destination on Long Island Sound and, by most accounts, one of the finest on the northeastern seaboard. The famous bascule drawbridge, the Mystic Seaport Museum walkable from your berth, and Abbott's Lobster in the Rough just a short dinghy ride away make this an unmissable stop for any sailor on the Sound.
