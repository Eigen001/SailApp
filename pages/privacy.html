Privacy Policy
Last updated: May 1, 2025

The short version: we collect what we need to match photos to your vessel and operate the platform. We do not sell your personal data or share your GPS position publicly. You can delete your vessel profile and all associated data at any time.

1. Information We Collect

When you register a vessel on SailSnap, we collect vessel documentation data (MMSI, HIN, USCG documentation number, state registration number), vessel name, hailing port, hull description, and reference photographs you upload.

We collect your email address for account management and photo alert notifications. We do not require your legal name, physical address or payment information beyond what is necessary to process your subscription through our secure payment processor (Stripe).

  • Vessel identification data (MMSI, HIN, registration numbers)
  • Reference photos you upload of your vessel
  • Email address for account and alerts
  • GPS position data from AIS sync (if enabled)
  • Photos uploaded to the community feed with metadata
2. How We Use Your Data

Your vessel data is used exclusively to train our AI identification model to recognize your vessel and match it against community-uploaded photos. Reference photos are processed by our computer vision system and stored securely.

Your email address is used to send photo match alerts, account notifications and (if you opt in) the weekly SailSnap community digest. We do not send promotional emails from third parties.

GPS and AIS position data is used to improve photo match accuracy by cross-referencing vessel position with photo upload location and timestamp. Your live position is never displayed publicly.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Ever.

Community-uploaded photos that are AI-matched to your vessel are visible to other registered SailSnap users. Your vessel's public profile (name, class, home harbor, community photos) is visible to all logged-in users. Your MMSI, HIN, email and GPS history are never visible publicly.

4. GPS & AIS Position Data

Position data from AIS sync is used only for photo matching accuracy. It is stored for 90 days and then deleted. Your current position is never displayed on any public map or shared with other users. You can disable AIS sync at any time in your vessel settings.

5. Data Deletion

You can delete your vessel profile and all associated data at any time from your account settings. Deletion is permanent and completed within 30 days. Community photos you uploaded of other vessels are not deleted when you delete your account, but your association with those photos is removed.

6. Contact

Privacy questions: privacy@sailsnap.app. We aim to respond within 2 business days.
